About ContNeXt

ContNeXt is web application that allows the exploration of comprehensive context-specific portraits of biological processes using gene expression data, and the change in such portraits across different contexts. The variability in such portraits gives rise to the diverse functional behaviors of biological systems.

ContNeXt is developed and maintained in an academic capacity by the Fraunhofer SCAI Department of Bioinformatics.
